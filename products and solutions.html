Battery solutions

The battery pack of the EVOGO system is known as the choco-SEB (Swapping Electric Block). It is a mass-produced battery designed specifically for EV battery swapping. With a tiny size and simplistic form, this modular battery block has a high gravimetric (Wh/Kg) and volumetric energy density. If the user utilises more than one battery block, the block incorporates a wireless BMS that can connect wirelessly with other battery blocks.
